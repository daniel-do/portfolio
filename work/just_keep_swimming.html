Just Keep Swimming

An endless runner game where the user controls a swimming fish, avoiding kelp obstacles that appear as the fish swims increasingly faster.

Development, Art, Design: Daniel Do

Sprite Art: Collin Lucich

Made for the CMPM 120: Game Development class at UCSC, Fall 2023.
