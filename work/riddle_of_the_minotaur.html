Riddle of the Minotaur

A puzzle game where the user traverses through procedurally generated mazes and answers riddles via dragons that are blocking the user's path.

Developed the game called "Riddle of the Minotaur" shown in the TV show "Batman: The Animated Series" Season 1, Episode 41 titled "If You're So Smart, Why Aren't You Rich?"

Made for the CMPM 120: Game Development Experience class at UCSC, Fall 2023.
